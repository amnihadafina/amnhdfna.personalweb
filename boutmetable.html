About Me

Hi and Assalamualaikum! My name is Amni Hadafina binti Abd Ghani. I was born on January 5, 2004, at Hospital Tuanku Fauziah, Kangar, Perlis, and I come from the smallest yet most charming state in Malaysia—Perlis Indera Kayangan. Born and raised in this serene state, I’ve always felt a deep connection to its peaceful vibe.

I come from a loving family of six, including my parents. As the eldest of four siblings, I’ve embraced my role as the big sister with pride and responsibility. My favorite color? Black! It’s my go-to for almost everything because it gives me a sense of comfort and confidence.

When it comes to personality, I’d describe myself as a happy-go-lucky person who cherishes nature and adventure. I often challenge myself to step out of my comfort zone, trying new things to sharpen my soft skills and broaden my horizons.

Currently, I’m pursuing my Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Merbok, Kedah. If all goes as planned, I’ll be graduating in 2025—InshaAllah! My diploma journey has been a rollercoaster of highs and lows, but every moment has been truly rewarding. I’m incredibly grateful for the unwavering support of my family and friends, who motivate me to keep pushing forward. Alhamdulillah a’la kulli hal :)
