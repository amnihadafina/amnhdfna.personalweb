My Leadership and Responsibilities in IMAN

Since my second semester, I have been actively involved in the IMAN association, one of the Islamic organizations at UiTM Kedah. IMAN’s mission is to spread da’wah among the students of UiTM Kedah. Through this association, I have been able to develop valuable soft skills such as management, communication, and problem-solving.

IMAN has also given me the opportunity to connect with many kind and inspiring individuals. I am surrounded by friends who consistently remind each other of "Amar Ma’ruf Nahi Munkar", creating a positive vibe of "Biah Solehah".

During my time with IMAN, I was given the opportunity to hold several leadership roles, including:

2024/2025

  • Deputy Vice President of IMAN for the 24/25 Session - Assisting the president in managing and coordinating activities.
  • Head of Muslimat for the IMAN members - Leading the Muslimat members in the organization.

2024

  • Secretary of the Nuzul Al-Quran Program 2024 - Took minutes and prepared program reports for UiTM Kedah.
  • Deputy Naqibah of Nuqaba’ Iman Program 23/24 - Scheduled monthly usrah sessions and organized activities.
  • Head of the Quran Recitation Group organized by IMAN Club 2024 - Led Quran recitation sessions during Ramadan.
  • Head of Registration and Souvenir Unit for the Kembara PU Yeop Program 2024 - Managed budgets for registration and souvenirs.
  • Head Exco of Economics in IMAN Club 23/24 - Managed the club's funds and budget for economic activities.
  • Technical Committee for the Annual Grand Meeting IMAN 2024 - Assessed venues for suitability based on technical needs.

2023

  • Protocol Committee for the Tazkirah Nuzul Al-Quran Program - Directed ushers and protocol officers for guest seating and flow management.
  • Committee for the Ihya’ Ramadhan Program - Distributed iftar meals for students at UiTM Kedah.
